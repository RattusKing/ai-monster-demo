Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

1. Introduction

EchoSprite ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Discord Authentication Data

When you log in with Discord, we collect:

  • Discord User ID
  • Username and discriminator
  • Avatar URL
  • Server/guild memberships (if you use group viewing features)

2.2 Content You Upload

We store:

  • Avatar images (in base64 format)
  • Avatar settings and configurations

2.3 Technical Data

We automatically collect:

  • IP address (for rate limiting and security)
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage analytics (page views, feature usage)
  • Error logs and diagnostics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the EchoSprite service
  • Authenticate your identity via Discord
  • Store and display your avatar configurations
  • Enable voice channel integration features
  • Improve our service through usage analytics
  • Prevent abuse and ensure security
  • Communicate service updates or issues

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your data on secure servers provided by Render.com (backend) and GitHub Pages (frontend). Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database with industry-standard security measures.

Security measures include:

  • Encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Secure session management
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Regular security updates
  • Input validation and sanitization

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Discord: For authentication purposes via OAuth2
  • Hosting Providers: Render.com and GitHub (infrastructure)
  • Law Enforcement: If required by law or to protect our rights

When you use group viewing features, other users in the same Discord voice channel may see your username and avatar as configured in Discord.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use EchoSprite. If you wish to delete your account and data, please contact us through our GitHub repository. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance or security purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Stop using the service at any time

8. Cookies and Local Storage

We use session cookies for authentication and local storage for temporary caching. These are essential for the service to function. By using EchoSprite, you consent to this use.

9. Children's Privacy

EchoSprite is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy with an updated "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us through our GitHub repository: github.com/RattusKing/echosprite
